Fitness Equipment

2 - 24kg Adjustable Dumbbell

With adjustable dumbbells, you can switch between
different weights to give your muscles a surprise. Functional, full-body strength for your legs, back, chest, abs, shoulders and arms.

Resistance Bands

Resistance Bands are affordable and easy to store. Resistance bands can be just as effective as dumbbells. Used for stretching and mobility. It improves strength and stabilization.

Kettlebells

Holding weights by a handle engages your arms, legs, shoulders, back and abdominal muscles. The pull on your muscles can strengthen them and it improves posture as well.

Exercise ball

Increases lumbar mobility. Increases abdominal and back muscle strength. Increases balance and stability. Develops the overall control and strength of the core body muscles.

Fitness Accessories

Fitness Roller

Helps relieve muscle tightness, soreness, inflammation and increases your joint range of motion. A foam roller can be effective to add to your warm up or cooldown, before or after a workout.

Exercise Mat

Helps stabilize your body and prevents you from slipping on a slick floor. The cushion is to prevent aches and pains while you exercise.

Recovery and Self Care

Massage Gun

Used for if you want to reduce muscle tension. Reduces soreness or stiffness from workouts. Delivers a quick hit of relief to any part of your body with a deep massage.

Consistency Is Key

In order to maximize the benefits of fitness, it would be a good practice to
incorporate daily exercise and physical activities.

It is essential to choose activities that suit individual preference
and health conditions to enjoy a longer and healthier life
